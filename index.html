FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Q1.What services are included in your construction projects?

Our construction services include project planning, site preparation, building construction, electrical and plumbing installation, interior finishing, and final inspections. We manage each phase from start to finish.

Q2.How long does a typical construction project take?

Project duration varies depending on the size and complexity. On average, residential projects take 3–6 months, while commercial buildings may take 6–12 months or longer.

Q3.Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we manage all necessary building permits and coordinate inspections with local authorities to ensure everything is up to code throughout the construction process.

Q4.Can you help with design and architecture?

Absolutely. We work closely with architects and designers to bring your vision to life. Whether you need full architectural services or just design consultation, we can assist.

Q5.What types of construction projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in residential homes, commercial buildings, office spaces, retail stores, and renovation or remodeling projects. No matter the scale, we bring expert craftsmanship to every job.
